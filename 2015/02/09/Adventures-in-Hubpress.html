Adventures in Hubpress

Things just got a little easier…​

A little while back Github introduce Github Pages, where you could easily use your free Github account to host HTML pages. Well today I discovered Hubpress which takes that concept a step further and gives you a free blogging system that just uses your free Github hosting.

Now there are defintely other tools out there that let you do something similar, Jeckyl comes to mind. But Hubpress makes it really easy with a nice clean editor and a live preview window. Once you are done with your post, click Publish and you are good to go!
